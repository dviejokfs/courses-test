# Verifier changed

A verifier is an entity that verifies the authenticity of a verifiable credential. The verifier is responsible for checking the digital signature on the credential to ensure that it was issued by a trusted issuer. The verifier is also responsible for checking that the claims in the credential are accurate and up-to-date.
